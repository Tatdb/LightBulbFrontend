The Challenge

  1. There are N number of light bulbs lined up in a row in a long room. Each bulb has its own switch and is currently switched off.
  2. The room has an entry door and an exit door. There are N number of people lined up outside the entry door.
  3. Each bulb is numbered consecutively from 1 to 100. Each person person is also numbered consecutively from 1 to 100.
  4. Person No. 1 enters the room, switches on every bulb, and exits.
  5. Person No. 2 enters and flips the switch on every second bulb (turning off bulbs 2, 4, 6, ...).
  6. Person No. 3 enters and flips the switch on every third bulb (changing the state on bulbs 3, 6, 9, ...).
  7. This continues until the total, N number of people have passed through the room.
